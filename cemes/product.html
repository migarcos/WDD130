MS SEALANTS

The latest technology in sealants

FIXALL HIGH TACK

Super strong sealant and adhesive with high initial tack and end strength (320kg/10cm²). Based on SMX Polymer Technology, High Tack achieves high performance flexible bonding and sealing of all types of materials onto almost any surface, both porous and non porous, even if wet or humid.

Applications

For all bonding, sealing and filling applications where initial grab and final bond strength is important. Heavy duty bonding and sealing in all weather conditions, on most surfaces. Also suitable for bonding porous natural stone surfaces, such as marble and limestone. Sanitary sealing and bonding.
